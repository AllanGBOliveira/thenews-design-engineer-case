We've handpicked some of Brazil's **lesser-known destinations** that make you question why anyone would pay a fortune to visit Santorini when we have this right here.

::section-label{label="DESTINATIONS OF THE WEEK"}

- 🌊 **Jalapão (TO)** — orange sand dunes and crystal-clear natural springs. Brazil's Atacama.
- 🏔️ **Serra do Cipó (MG)** — waterfalls, trails and starry skies 100km from Belo Horizonte.
- 🌴 **Ilha do Marajó (PA)** — buffaloes on the beach and sunsets at the meeting of the waters.
- 🎭 **Ouro Preto (MG)** — baroque in the air, cachaça on the table and history in every stone.

> Editor's tip: travel off-season. Same landscapes, half the price, zero crowds.
