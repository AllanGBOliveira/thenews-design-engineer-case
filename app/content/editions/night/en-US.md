**King Charles has decided to open his wallet.** For the first time in the history of the British monarchy, a king has revealed how much tax he pays: **£12.9 million** in 2024–2025 alone, placing Charles among the UK's top 100 taxpayers.

Like father, like son… Prince William declared **£7.76 million** for the same period. Together, they've handed over more than **£50 million** to HMRC since 2022 — and that's just the tax. Imagine how much they spend on tea.

> The decision to disclose their tax figures is an attempt to improve the royal family's image after years of scandals — from **Harry & Meghan** to Charles's own health issues.
