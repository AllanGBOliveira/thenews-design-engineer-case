## Good morning, Brazil.

Brazil's Ibovespa closed higher on Thursday, briefly surpassing the 173,000-point mark at its daily high. While markets digested the latest domestic inflation figures, the spotlight fell on **Braskem**, whose shares plummeted after the petrochemical company sought court protection from creditors.

> The **IPCA-15** — Brazil's preview inflation indicator — came in at +0.41% for June, showing a deceleration from the prior month. The figure came in slightly below market expectations (0.44%), which should give the Central Bank some breathing room heading into the next Copom meeting.
